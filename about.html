My Story

5+ years. Two continents. One consistent result: pipeline that converts.

I'm Muhammad Aashar Tanveer, a B2B business development specialist based in Lahore, Pakistan, working with companies across North America, Europe and MENA. I started in international B2B sales in 2020 and have since carried quota for a US e-learning company, beaten a $350K annual number selling lab-automation SaaS into Western Europe and North America, and now source acquisition targets for Perseus Group — part of Constellation Software, one of the world's largest software acquirers.

What makes my approach different is where I came from: a research background (MPhil in Molecular Biology). I treat prospecting like research — understand the account, find the trigger, say something relevant. It's slower per message and dramatically faster per booked meeting.

Today I offer that same quota-carrying skill set to businesses as a freelance partner: lead generation, cold outreach, appointment setting and full dedicated-SDR engagements. Fluent in English and Urdu.
